# Zamzam Journey - Section Images Reference

Export settings: **JPG, 80% quality, 1920x1080 (desktop) / 1080x1920 (portrait)**

---

## THE NARRATIVE ARC

The journey follows Prophet Ibrahim/Hajar's Zamzam story:
1. Ibrahim gave → 2. Now he thirsts → 3. Ummah runs to help → 4. Be his Zamzam

---

## SECTION 1: STORY (The Oasis - He Gave)

**Arabic:** أعطى الماء
**English:** HE GAVE WATER
**Meaning:** "He gave water" - Ibrahim (scholar) gave knowledge/spiritual nourishment to the Ummah for 20 years

**Visual direction:** Abundant oasis, falcon flying free, palm trees, blue water - ABUNDANCE before the trial

**Files needed:**
- `section-story-desktop.jpg` (1920x1080) ✅ DONE
- `section-story-portrait.jpg` (1080x1920) ✅ DONE

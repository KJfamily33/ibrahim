# Quick Video Submission Form Setup

## Option 1: Google Form (Recommended)

Create a simple Google Form with these fields:

1. **Your Name** (Short answer, required)
2. **Your Title/Position** (Short answer - e.g., "Imam at Islamic Center of...")
3. **Your City/Country** (Short answer)
4. **Upload Your Video** (File upload, required)
   - Allow: Video files only
   - Max size: 100MB
5. **Permission to Share** (Checkbox, required)
   - "I give permission to use this video to support Brother Ibrahim's campaign"
6. **Your Social Media** (Short answer, optional)
   - "So we can tag you when sharing"

**Form settings:**
- Collect email addresses: Yes
- Allow file uploads: Yes
- Confirmation message: "JazakAllahu Khayran! Your endorsement means the world to Brother Ibrahim. We'll be in touch soon."

**Share the form link** instead of asking them to email/WhatsApp the video.

## Option 3: Simple WhatsApp

Just give them your number. They record and send.
- Pro: Everyone knows how to use WhatsApp
- Con: You have to organize/download videos manually

---

## Recommendation

**Start with WhatsApp** (zero setup, works now)
**Graduate to Google Form** if you get more than 5-10 endorsements coming in
